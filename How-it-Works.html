How it Works


The concept is very simple you walk around Aarhus searching for street art.

As you find art take a photos while standing in front of it.

We will then record that you found and collected it.

As you do this, you unlock and add progress to certain badges and titles that you can

display to your friends. Have fun!



If ever in doubt press this little icon and it
will tell you what to do on each page!
